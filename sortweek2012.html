SoRT Week 12-13

One of the most awaited events of the college, ‘THE SoRT WEEK’ commenced on the 27th of August and continued will great success till 1st of September. Stalls were arranged on the ground, second and fifth floor from 10 am to 3.30 pm. The basic motto of the SoRT week was to help in selling products made by physically and mentally challenged and thereby helping the organizations that represented them. Participation of staff along with students beefed up the motivational spirit of SoRT(Social Responsibility Team). SoRT achieving its goals was beneficial to three organizations namely:- SELF-ESTEEM, ADHAR, and NASEOH. Paper bags, gift and ceramic articles, table cloth, clutches, purses made by the people associated with SELF-ESTEEM (Chembur) were sold. ADHAR located in Badlapur was helped greatly by SoRT through sale of products like candles, purses etc. NASEOH (Chembur) provided SoRT with floating candles, ceramic articles, bags, clutches, folders and notepads. Besides, chocolates from NASEOH was the most attractive and achieved maximum sales during the SoRT week. Good quality assignment papers were made available to students at a reasonable price. The students contributed in large numbers to the ‘BOOKS, CLOTHES AND STATIONERY COLLECTION DRIVE’ which were then donated to Vedanta Foundation (Chembur) and MaaNiketan (Thane). Besides, the ‘RECYCLE KARO’ in Airoli was assisted by the students of the college in collecting waste paper through SoRT. To create social awareness, SoRT displayed social awareness PowerPoint presentations on the ground floor. SoRT also displayed a slideshow of all its events conducted over the last two years.
