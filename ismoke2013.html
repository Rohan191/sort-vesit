Lung cancer is the major cause of cancer deaths among both men and women and 90 percent of lung cancer deaths are due to smoking. Techfest, IIT Bombay took this initiative with a motto to enlighten the youth and show them the right path. Main aim of the campaign was interacting with all the students, informing them with the help of oncologist and involving them in this initiative through a signature campaign and a Doodle Project so as to encourage students to support the motive of creating a sound future of our society. SoRT urged students to express themselves and show their support to this campaign by sharing their message or an illustration on the design provided. The initiative was conducted in around 12 cities and 50 colleges in all. In Mumbai, various colleges participated in the event including VESIT. The event was a successful one with over 900+ doodles and 1000+ visitors from VESIT and VES group of colleges.
