SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY TEAM - VESIT


The Social Responsibility Team (SoRT) of Vivekanad Education Society's Institute of Technology (VESIT) was initially a part of the technical festival of the college 'Praxis' and used to be functional only during the festive season in VESIT. However due to impressive work by the seniors the principal included SoRT in the Students' Council of the college from academic year 2011-2012. Since then SoRT has conducted a plethora of social activities. This is the third year of SoRT as a part of the Students' council. The team continues to work relentlessly for the betterment of the society. The Social Responsibility Week also called the SoRT Week is is the highlight of all the events SoRT conducts throughout the year. SoRT has also adopted a village called 'Chinchpada' in the outskirts of Mumbai and has conducted various events in the village.

The SoRT motto is -

"BE THE WINGS , THE WORLD NEEDS"
