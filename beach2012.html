Beach Cleaning Drive

SoRT in collaboration with seven Rotaract clubs spread across Mumbai arranged the clean-up drive on 30th September 2012, Sunday, at Juhu beach. This event was held on a day next to Ganapati Visarjan with a motto to maximize the cleaning of beach. The event was open for all the college students. Around 90 socially active students along with the SRIs ( SoRT Incharge) participated to make the event a grand one and elevate it to a greater level.. The event started at around 8:30 am at Juhu Beach, Vile Parle. All the volunteers were given a pair of disposable gloves and a trash bag to collect the waste they found. Being a day after Ganapati Visarjan, maximum dumped waste were found littered on the beach, including Plaster Of Paris (one of the most dangerous substance which takes a toll on the aquatic life), plastic materials and flowers. All the participants showed a lot of enthusiasm in cleaning the beach. With around 2.5-3 hrs of hard-work the VESITians managed to clean-up quite a large section of the beach. A large amount of waste was gathered and then disposed off safely with due precautions. After the cleaning session was over, the participants were provided with refreshments.
